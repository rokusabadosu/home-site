the place where we met

Even now, after all these years, I can close my eyes and be back there in a heartbeat. Just a boy on the shore of my home town, transfixed by the sight of the world tree blooming on the opposite shore. An impossible eruption in shades of pink and flame and life at a scale that dwarfs the mind. A quiet footstep to my left and I turned. And there you were.

This short story was inspired by this beautiful image from aeonicmoon and the song of the same name by Kaiyo.
